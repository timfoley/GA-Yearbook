UXing This Thing

#The Process
1. Define the problem
1. Discovery/Research
  * user interviews/survey
  * landscape analysis (how do other people solve the problem?)
1. Sketching and Ideation
  * Design Studio
    * rapid sketching (collaborative?)
  * Further sketches
1. Narrowing the Scope
  * Scenarios
  * User flows/stories
  * wireframing
1. Prototyping and Testing
